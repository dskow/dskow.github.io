    How to Play

    Every puzzle has exactly one solution, and it can always be found by pure deduction — no guessing needed. Each person in the tale matches exactly one item in every other category. Use the clues to mark the grid: ✕ where a pairing is impossible, ● where it is certain. Placing a ● automatically crosses off the rest of its row and column in that block. When you know the answer to the question, declare it — a wrong declaration costs 30 seconds, and consulting the Oracle costs 45. Fastest times enter the Cvrsvs Honorvm.
